This Privacy Policy explains what Numbox ("we", "us"), operated by [Legal entity name], collects, why, and your choices. We designed the service to collect as little personal data as possible.

What we collect

  • Account data: your email address and a securely hashed password. We never store your password in readable form.
  • Payment data: deposits are processed by a third-party cryptocurrency payment processor. We receive transaction metadata (amount, currency, status, an invoice identifier) but not your wallet keys. We do not store full card numbers; the service is crypto-funded.
  • Rental and message data: the numbers you rent, rental times, and the contents of SMS messages delivered to those numbers during your rental term, so you can read them in your inbox and via the API.
  • Technical logs: limited request logs (such as timestamps and coarse usage) used to operate, secure, and debug the service and to detect abuse.

How we use it

  • To provide the service: deliver SMS to your inbox, process rentals, maintain your balance.
  • To secure the service: detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our Acceptable Use Policy.
  • To communicate with you about your account and support requests.
  • To meet legal obligations.

What we do not do

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use the contents of your SMS messages for advertising. We do not share your data with third parties except the service providers below or where required by law.

Service providers

We rely on a small number of processors to run Numbox, including a cloud/edge hosting provider, a managed database provider, and a cryptocurrency payment processor. They process data only to provide their service to us.

Retention

SMS message contents are retained only as long as needed to provide the inbox and are routinely deleted after your rental term ends (and within a short window thereafter). Account and minimal transaction records are kept as long as your account is active and as required for legal, accounting, and anti-fraud purposes. You may request deletion of your account as described below.

Security

Passwords are hashed, transport is encrypted (HTTPS), and access to systems is restricted. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your data.

Your choices

You can request access to or deletion of your account data by emailing [support email]. Some records may be retained where we are legally required to keep them. Because a rented number is shared infrastructure used by others before and after you, do not use it to receive messages you would consider highly sensitive beyond ordinary verification codes.

Changes

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date reflects the latest version.
