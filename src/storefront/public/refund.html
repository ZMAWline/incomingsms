Refund Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

Terms Privacy Acceptable Use Refunds

We want the service to work for you. This policy explains when a rental is refundable and how to get help.

Deposits become balance

When you fund your account, the amount becomes prepaid balance (store credit) usable for rentals. Balance does not expire and can be used at any time. Balance is not withdrawable to cash or back to a wallet, and is not transferable between accounts.

Rentals

  • Once a number is delivered to you, the rental is non-refundable. At that point you have received exactly what you paid for — a working number and inbox for the term.
  • Faulty number: if a number you rented is genuinely not functioning during your rental window (for example it cannot receive SMS at all), contact support within the rental term and we will investigate and, where the fault is on our side, credit your balance for the affected rental or provide a replacement number.
  • We do not guarantee that any specific third-party website, app, bank, or service will accept a given number. A platform refusing or blocking a number is not by itself a fault that entitles you to a refund, though we welcome the report so we can improve our inventory.

How to request help

Email support@otpdock.io from your account email with the number, the rental time, and a description of the problem. We aim to respond promptly. Approved remedies are issued as account balance credit.

Chargebacks

Because the service is funded with cryptocurrency, transactions are final once confirmed on-chain. Attempting to reverse a confirmed payment, or opening a fraudulent dispute, is a violation of our Terms and may result in account termination.

Account closure for cause

If we terminate an account for violating our Acceptable Use Policy or for fraud, remaining balance may be forfeited.

Contact

Refund questions: support@otpdock.io.
